Grayscale Image

Line drawings/ Black & White Images

This model can be applied to Gray scale images. Gray scale is a range of shades of gray without apparent color.


Colored Image

RGB class of Image

This model can be applied to images that includes color information for each pixel. A color image has three values RGB.


Hatched Image

Images with hatching lines

This model will give a lower height to the hatching lines and a higher height to the outer lines.
